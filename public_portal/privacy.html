Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

Your patient data never leaves your computer.

FEDRAL.AI is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. We use federated learning technology that allows AI models to learn from your data without ever seeing it.

What We Collect

❌ What We DON'T Collect

  • Patient names, IDs, or any identifiable information
  • Raw patient data (biomarkers, test results, etc.)
  • Hospital location or address details
  • Email addresses or contact information (unless you provide them voluntarily)
  • Any Protected Health Information (PHI)

✅ What We DO Collect (With Your Consent)

  • Hospital Name - Only the name you provide during registration
  • Model Updates - Anonymous mathematical weights (numbers only)
  • Accuracy Metrics - How well the model performed on your data
  • Sample Count - Number of patients analyzed (count only, no data)
  • Agent Version - Which version of the software you're using

How Federated Learning Works

🏥

Step 1: Local Training

Your data stays on your computer. The AI model trains locally using your patient data.

→
📊

Step 2: Extract Weights

Only the model's learned patterns (mathematical weights) are extracted. No patient data.

→
🔒

Step 3: Encrypted Upload

With your consent, encrypted weights are sent to our server. Still no patient data.

→
🌐

Step 4: Global Model

Weights from all hospitals are combined to improve the global model for everyone.

Your Control & Consent

🎯 Explicit Consent

Every time you contribute, we ask for your explicit permission. You'll see exactly what will be shared before it happens.

👁️ See Results First

You always see your analysis results before being asked to contribute. No surprises.

🚫 Opt-Out Anytime

You can use the agent completely offline. Contribution is 100% optional.

🗑️ Right to Withdraw

Contact us anytime to remove your hospital's contributions from our system.

Data Security

  • Encryption - All uploads use AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Transport - HTTPS/TLS for all network communication
  • No Storage - We don't store patient data because we never receive it
  • Access Control - Only authorized personnel can access aggregated metrics
  • Regular Audits - Third-party security audits conducted annually

Compliance

HIPAA

Compliant with Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act. No PHI is transmitted or stored.

GDPR

Compliant with General Data Protection Regulation. Explicit consent, right to erasure, data minimization.

SOC 2

Type II certified for security, availability, and confidentiality.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

The only data we collect (anonymous model weights) is used solely to improve the FEDRAL.AI global model. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable statistics (e.g., "100 hospitals participating") for research purposes.

Your Rights

  • Right to Access - Request a copy of any data we have about your hospital
  • Right to Erasure - Request deletion of your hospital's contributions
  • Right to Object - Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Portability - Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent - Stop contributing at any time
