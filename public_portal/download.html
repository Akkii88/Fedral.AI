Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes! The FEDRAL.AI agent is completely free for hospitals. No credit card, no subscription, no hidden fees.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required. Just download the file and double-click to run. It's a standalone application.

What data format is required?

A CSV file with patient data (age, gender, biomarkers, etc.). We provide a sample file and format guide in the agent.

Is my patient data safe?

Absolutely. All analysis happens on your computer. Patient data never leaves your machine. We're HIPAA compliant.

Do I have to share my results?

No. Sharing is completely optional. You can use the agent offline and keep all results private.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our federated model achieves 85-90% accuracy, trained on data from 100+ hospitals. Accuracy improves over time.
